Underwriting Rules

In this Form, Business users can define one or more pre-defined rules to enforce validations. All these rules get evaluated as "OR" condition, but business users have authority to define "AND" logic between two or more rules. Business has complete control to define the error type and messages which are going to be appear on UI for end users. Business has an authority even creating new rule by using interactive chat bot because of any adverse situations like Fire, Riot, Flood etc or due to ever evolving business scenarios.
